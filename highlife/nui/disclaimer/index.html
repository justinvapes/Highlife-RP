Help & Administration

If you require help or advice at any point, be sure to ask in game using /ooc. If you're still stuck, join our Discord and check if your problem/query falls under any of our chat channels. If that fails contact a member of staff, preferably a Moderator first!


Roleplay

HighLife is a roleplay server, common sense is required to play along with a working microphone, a full list of our rules can be found on our Discord #basic-info channel.


Bug, Reports & Suggestions

If you encounter an issue when playing, whether a major or minor glitch report it to our Discord #feedback channel, for suggestions refer to the #suggestions channel but please follow the format! If you find a person you suspect of breaking the rules then make sure to report them to any staff online or join Discord and let a member of staff know.
